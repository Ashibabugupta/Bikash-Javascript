WHAT IS SEMANTIC?

SEMANTIC HTML OR SEMANTIC MARKUP IS HTML
THAT INTRODUCES MEANING TO THE WEB PAGE RATHER THAN JUST PRESENTATION.

EXAMPLE OF SEMANTIC TAG ARE FORM,TABLE,ARTICLE,ASIDE,STYLE ETC.

Semantics is the study of meaning that is used to understand human expresstion through language.
Semantics largely determine our reading comprehension,how we understand others,
and even what decision we make as a result of our interpretations.
